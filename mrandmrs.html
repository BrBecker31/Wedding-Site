The Beckers

We met on October 5, 2014, at OHSO, a local Brewery. During the first few dates we got to know each other but it wasn’t until Piwo (pee-vo) first called Bryan “Dad” we both understood how far our relationship could grow. We continued to spend time together hiking, traveling and cooking for one another; we also added another pup to our family, Moose (Moosifer – depending on how he behaved that day). On December 28, 2015, Bryan proposed while skiing at Stevens Pass in Washington. We will exchange vows and celebrate our love with friends and family on October 14, 2017.
